The Everyday Illusion

Debunking common health myths

"White bread vs Brown bread — which actually wins for everyday health?"
Tap to reveal
"White bread can be just as okay — brown color isn't proof of whole grain. Whole-grain label > color. Check fiber content."
Many brown breads are just white bread with coloring. Look for "100% whole grain" on the label and check for at least 3g fiber per serving.
"Maida (refined flour) vs Whole wheat — is whole wheat always better?"
Tap to reveal
"Usually whole wheat wins — but only if it's whole. Some 'wheat' breads are refined. Read ingredient list: 'whole' matters."
Products labeled simply as "wheat" may contain refined flour. Always check that "whole wheat" appears as the first ingredient.
